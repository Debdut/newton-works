      • The entire document is in considerable disorder, with frequent disjunctures in the sense. Where possible, the pages are here transcribed in what appears to be the intended sequence, but in many cases it has been impossible to establish any such sequence. The text breaks off in mid-sentence on the final page (f. 49r).
