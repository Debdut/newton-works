West Rid Com Eboy SsThe Information of Unidentified Constable of Austwick in this Riding taken beofre me Unidentified Esquire &ct. upon oath this 30.th day of Aug.t 1703.

Who upon his Oath saith and Informeth that being com̄anded by a warrant under the hands and Seales of the Justices of the peace (in the ,receding Information Men{t}ioned[Editorial Note 1]) did with other persons apprehend Michall Lawson John Airey alias Verey and Unidentified, accused for Counterfeiting Stampt paper, and also fro buying Dyes for Counterfeiting Crowns and halfe Crowns, and for Counterfeiting the currant coyn of this kingdom, he with others did apprehend the Said Unidentified and Unidentified, (who when apprehended being asked his name by this Informant said it was Unidentified) and also the said Unidentified was at the same time taken upon suspicion at or near Michall Lawsons Smithy, and going afterwards to Search for Unidentified at Ham̄ond head, togeather with one Mr Unidentified (who brought the Warrant) upon Searching the house he saw the said Mr Unidentified, he saw him the Said Unidentified finde therein a Dye or Stamp for counterfeiting Stamp paper which was for the double Sixpenny Stamps, as also another for a Single Counterfeit Sixpenny Stamp, with some Sort of Metall which this Informant doth not understand, and further saith that the Said Mr Unidentified delivered the same into this Informants hands Capt. jurat &ct. coram                      Unidentified Unidentified

[Editorial Note 1] See Deposition No. 475.
