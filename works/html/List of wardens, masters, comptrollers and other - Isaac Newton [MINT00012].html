1603. Sir Tho. Knyvett Warden. Sir Rich. Martin & his son Richd. Masters & Mr. Richd. Rogers Comptroller.
1607. The Lord Knyvett Warden. the rest the same.
1611. The Lord Knyvett, & Edmd. Dobleday Esqr. Wardens, the rest the same.
1619. Same Wardens; Sir Edwd. Villers Master & Worker. Richd. Rogers Comptroller
1621. Henry Tweddy Esqr. Warden. the rest the same.
1624. Sir Edw. Villiers & Sir Wm. Parkhurst Wardens, Sir Randall Cranfield Master.
1626. Same Wardens, no master, andrew palmer assaymaster.
1627. Sir Wm. Parkhurst Warden, Sir Rob. Harley Master. Rich. Rogers Comptroller
1638. Sir Wm. Parkhurst Warden, Hen. Cogan Comptroller. no master. Sir Ralph freeman, & Sir Tho. Aylesbury Masters of request.
1644. Sir Robt. Harley by ordinance of parliament 6t. may 1643. directed to be master & Worker, Warden & comptroller the same.
1657. Aaron Gourdain Dr. of phisick master, Iohn St. Iohn Warden Tho. Barnardiston Comptroller authorised by the Keepers of the liberty of England by authority of parliament in 1649.
1663. Sir Ralph freeman master, Sir Wm. Parkhurst Warden, Iames Hoare Comptroller. Warrant to Hen. Slingbey master & Worker to coin Groats & three pence 19 Ian. 1662.
1669. Hen Slingbey by Warrant being master, after the death of Sir Ra{l}ph freeman, Sir Anth. Sellinger Warden, & Iam. Hoare Comptroller
1681. Sir Iohn Buckworth, Char. Duncomb, & Iam. Hoare officiating as master Sir anth. St. leger, Sir Tho. Wharton, and his son Philip being Warden.
1684. Philip Wharton Warden. Same commission.
1686. Tho. Neale, Char. Duncomb, & Iam. Hoare as masters, and Sir Philip floyd Warden.
1692. Benj. Overton Warden.
