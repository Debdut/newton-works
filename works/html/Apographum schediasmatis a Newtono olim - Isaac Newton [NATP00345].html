a Newtono olim scripti
cui forte appositum fuit scribendi tempus 13 Novem. 1665.

Probleme

An equation being given expressing the Relation of two or more lines x, y & z described in the same time by two or more moving bodies A, B, C, & to find the relation of their velocities p, q, r, &c

Solution

Set all the terms on one side of the Equation that they become equal to nothing. Multiply each term by so many times pxpx as x hath dimensions in that term. Secondly multiply each term by so many times qyqy as y hath dimensions in it. Thirdly multiply each term by so many times rzrz as z hath dimensions in it &c. The summ of all these products shall be equal to nothing. Which Equation gives the relation of p, q, r, &c.

Demonstration

If two bodies A & B move uniformly, the one succesively from a to c, e, g &c the other in the same times from b to d, f, h &c then are the lines ac & bd, ce & df, eg & fh &c in the same proportion to one another as are the velocities p & q wherewith the bodies describe them <2r> And tho they move not uniformly yet are the infinitely little lines which each moment they describe, as the velocities are which they have while they describe them. As if the body A with the velocity p describe the infinitely little line po in one moment, the body B with the velocity q will describe the line in the same moment For p.q:::po. qo. So that if the described lines be x & y in one moment they will be x+po & y+qo in the next moment.
