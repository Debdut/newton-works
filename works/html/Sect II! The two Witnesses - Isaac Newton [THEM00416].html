Sect II.
The two Witnesses.

The Dragon & Beast have their proper churches true & false. The false Church of the Dragon is the two horned Beast as above & his true Church is the seven Churches of Asia. The fals church of the Beast is Whore of Babylon & his true Church is the two Churches called the two Witnesses For the Witnesses are called two Candlesticks & two Olive trees & Candlesticks signify Churches Apoc 1.20 & so do Olive trees Rom. 11.17 Therefore while the whole Empire continues undivided & is both Dragon & Beast, the Church of the whole Empire is both the seven Churches & the two Witnesses: But when the Empire becomes divided & the Dragon is put for the Greek Empire & the Beast for the Latin Empire, the seven Churches signify the true Church of God in the Eastern Empire & the two witnesses are his true Church in the western. For the Beast makes war with the two witnesses & overcomes them & kills them & their dead bodies lye in the streets of the great city Babylon & therefore they are in the western Empire & the seven Angels or Angels of the seven Churches sound the Trumpets & pour out the Vials or drink offerings at the seven sacrifices while the Empire continues divided, & therefore the seven Churches do not cease when the Woman separates from them & flyes into the wilderness, but continue in being & by consequence remain in the Dragons Kingdom .

<1v>

Now as the Dragon & Beast by the number of their heads & horns tally one another as a mark of their being originally { , } united: so the seven Churches of Asia & the Woman in the Wilderness tally{ . } one another in the number of Churches. For the heads of the Beast are called seven mountains where the woman sitteth & mountains are a type of royal Cities the seats of kingdoms & every kingdom has its Church. After the little horn of Daniels fourth Beast which is this woman, had in its rise {rooted} up three of the ten kings she reigned over the remaining seven ( such from their first number are also called ten ) & the seven Churches of these kingdoms answer in number to the seven Churches of Asia . Before she separated she was the seven Churches of Asia: by separation every Church became two, a true one & a false one & the seven false ones fled into the wilderness & reigned over the seven mountains in the form of a great Whore.

In like manner the two Witnesses & two horns of the second Beast tally one another as to the number of Churches. For as the Witnesses are two Churches so also the two horns of this beast are Churches being like the Lambs', seven horns which represent the seven Churches of Asia. Whence it seems to me that the two Witnesses & two horns of this Beast were originally united the two Witnesses being the sincere part of Christs visible Church from the beginning & the two horned Beast the dissemb{l}ing hypocritical part corrupt part called Nicolaitans & fals Apostles & the fals Prophet Balaam.. For as the ten horned Beast was from the beginning & his rising out of the sea signifies only his separating from the Dragon so the two horned Beast was from the beginning, being the Fals Prophet called Balaam whose doctrine is the doctrine of the Nicola{i}tans. & his rising out of the earth signifies only his separating from the two Witnesses. Now the Church of the whole Empire was distinguished from the beginning into Greeks & Latines & this distinction was improved by the building of Constantinople & dividing the Empire between this city & Rome & still further improved by divi{s}{d}ing the Empire between the sons of Constantine the Great: whereby the Woman received two wings of a great Eagle that She might fly into the Wilderness. And as the four wings of the Leopard signify the division of the kingdom of the Greeks into four kingdoms so the two wings of the Woman signify her division into the Greek & Latin Churches which churches are in different respects the two {w}itnesses & the two horns of the second Beast. So then as the first Beast had his heads & his wound before he rose out of the Sea so the second Beast had his two horns before he rose out of the earth & as the Dragon separated from the Beast and retired into the Eastern Empire without losing any of his horns so the second Beast separated & retired into the Eastern Empire without losing either of his horns, & so the two Witnesses continue to be called two notwithstanding their retiring into the west, there being a mixture of Greeks & Latines in both Empires & names & characters once imposed remaining to the end of the Prophesy.

So then as the Dragon & first Beast do each of them signify the whole Roman Empire from the beginning untill it becomes divided & then separaate from one another & signify the two parts: so the Seven Churches, the Woman, the two Witnesses & the two horned Beast are all of them from the beginning & do in several respects signify the Church of the whole Roman Empire untill it becomes divided into four parts two true Churches & two fals ones, & then separate from one another & signify those parts. Before the division the seven Churches & Woman do each of them signify the whole Church: the seven Churches have Nicolaitans among them & the Nicolaitans hold the doctrine of Balam the fals Prophet represented by the two horned {B}{b}east & they that oppose & testify against the Nicolaitans are the two Witnes{s}es.

Now as the separation of the ten horned Beast is represented by his rising out of the sea & that of the two horned Beast by his rising out of the earth, so the separation of the two witnesses is represented by the building of a new Temple

They also stand on the sea of glass in the Temple before the throne having gotten the victory over the Beast & over his Image by not worshipping them & by being sealed with the seal of God , & there they sing the song of Moses the servant of God & the song of the{e} Lamb with harps , that is, they prophesy & henceforward are the Prophets of God. . Sect 2.
The two Witnesses

When the Woman fled from the Temple of heaven on mount Sion into the Wilderness & left a remnant of her seed which keep the commandments of God: it is to be conceived that this remnant were the remnant of the seven churches & stayed in the Temple being the 144000 which were sealed out of the 12 Tribes & stand with the Lamb on mount Sion, before the throne & before the four animals & the Elders: & & continue to be represented by the seven candlesticks in this Temple. They are also the Victors which stand upon the sea of Glass in the same Temple. And the seven Angels which sound the Trumpets & pour out the vials of wrath are the Angels of this remnant of the seven Churches represented by the seven lamps in these candlesticks {F}{} It is also to be conceived that the Woman in fleeing from Mount Sion into the wilderness changed her Metropolis. She fled from the seven golden candlesticks which represent the seven churches of Asia her first Metropolis & in flying she went through the wilderness of Arabia to the great city called Babylon her new Metropolis. This Babylon is the seven hilled city opposed to the seven churches, the city Rome which to this day is the Metropolis of the Woman claims a supreme metropolitan power over all the Christian Churches. The seven hills or mountains where the woman sitteth are literally the seven hills on which Rome stood, but mystically they are the seven heads of the Beast that is they are the seven royal cities or head cities of the seven kingdoms represented by those seven horns of the Beast which remained after the little horn of Daniels fourth Beast had rooted up three of the ten first horns . For mountains in sacred prophesy signify cities, & the Spirit gives a double interpretation of the seven heads of the Beast calling them both mountains & kings, saying: Here is the mind that hath wisdome. The seven heads are seven mountains on which the Woman sitteth Also they are seven kings: five are fallen & one is & the other is not yet come &c. According to the first interpretation the seven heads are contemporary to one another like the four heads of Daniels Leopard & signify seven contemporary kingdoms, the seven mountains upon which the great City is seated being made a type of the imperial cities of these kingdoms & the seven churches of these seven cities are set in opposition to the seven Churches of the seven cities of Asia. According to the second interpretation the seven heads are seven successive dynasties of the Roman Empire five of which wer{e} fallen before the Beast rose out of the abyss or sea. In this last sense the heads belong more properly to the Dragon than to the Beast: in the other sense they belong more properly to the Beast than to the Dragon.

Now when the woman fled from the Temple on mount Sion & arrived at her place in the wilderness, it is to be conceived that a new Temple was built for her there. For the building of this temple is signified by the <6r> Angels saying to Iohn. Rise & measure the temple of God & the altar & them that worship therein, ( that {i}s their three courts ) but the court which is without the Temple leave out & measure it not{ . } for it is given unto the Gentiles & the {–} holy city shall they tread under foot forty & two months. And I will give power to my two witnesses & they shall prophesy 1260 {days} in sackcloth All this alludes to the Babylonians captivating the Iews, & treading Ierusalem under foot & to Ezekiels measuring the Temple in token that it s{hould} be rebuilt & to the building of the inner Temple in the reign of Cyrus & Darius, that is, the court of the House & the court of the altar & the court of the people called the weomens court, & leaving unbuilt the outward court which in Solomons Temple was the peoples Court but in the second Temple was given to the gentiles, & remained unbuilt all the reign of the Persians but the Gentiles came into it to worship both in the reign of the Persians while it remained unbuilt & also in the reign of the Greeks & Romans when it was built. It alludes also to the two Prophets Haggai & Zecchary who prophesied in the second Temple.

Now upon the final division of the Roman Empire into the Eastern & western Empires, as the Dragon which till then had signified the whole Empire retired into the east & {thence forward}{thenceforward} signified only the Eastern Empire & in its room the ten-horned Beast rose out of the earth to signify the western Empire, & as the woman which till then had signified the Church of the whole Empire fled from the Greek Empire into the wilderness & thenceforward signified only the national Church of the western Empire & in its room the two horned Beast rose out of the Earth to signify the {national}{rational} Church of the Eastern Empire: so the Temple of the Tabernacle in heaven with its Candlesticks & lamps all which till then had signified the true Church of God diffused throughout the whole Empire, retired from signifying the true Church in the western Empire & thenceforward signified only the true Church of God in the Eastern Empire, & in its room a second Temple is built with two Candlesticks to signify the true Church of God in the western Empire.

The two witnesses o{r} Prophets are therefore the people of God which worship in this Temple. For they are the two Candlesticks standing before the God of the earth, that is before his throne in the Temple: And in allusion to Zechary's [1] vision of two Olive trees emptying their oyle into the Candlesticks in the second Temple they are also called two olive trees. And the Candlesticks in the Temple signify the Churches of God who worship him in his Temple Apoc. 1.20, & { . } so do Olive trees Rom. 11.17, 24. They are the Churches of God in the western Empire because they are slain by the Beast, & lye dead in the streets of the great City, the city Babylon which in form of a woman rides upon the Beast.

If any man will hurt them fire proceedeth out of their mouth & devoureth their enemies . These have power over the waters to turn them to blood, & to smite the earth with all plagues as often as they will ; that is, these two Churches inflict the plagues of the seven trumpets. They joyn with the seven Angels or Angels of the seven Churches in smiting the earth wi{t}h all the seven plagues of the Trumpets & Vials of wrath, And therefore they worship in the second Temple all the time of th{e}{o}se plagues.

These two Churches are the mystical body of the mighty Angel which came down from heaven cloathed with a cloud & a rainbow upon his head & his face as it were the Sun & his feet as pillars of fire, & which had in his hand a little book open & set his right foot uponthe sea & left upon the earth, & cried with a loud voice. When all the seven seals of this book were opened then the son of man in the form of this Angel appears with it open in his hand to prophesy out of it & for that end hold{s} it open all the time that the seven thunders utter their voices, that is all the time that his mystical body the two Witnesses prophesy. As the seven eyes & seven horns of the <7r> Lamb represent the same thing with the seven lamps & seven candlesticks in the first Temple so the two eyes & two leggs of the son of man represent the same thing with the two lamps & two Candlesticks in the second Temple. And hence the witnesses are called two. By the building of Constantinople & division of the Roman Empire between the two cities of Rome & Constantinople & distinguishing the territories of those cities by the names of Earth & Sea. , the Woman received two wings of {a} great eagle upon which she flew into the wilderness, the two horned Beast received his two horns & the witnesses became two: for christ set his right foot upon the sea & his left upon the earth. And these characters being given t to the two-winged Woman, two-horned Beast & two Witnesses, before the final division of the Empire, they retained them after it As the ten-horned Beast rose out of the sea with seven heads, five of which were fallen & one of them wounded before he rose so The two horned Beast rose out of the earth with two horns which grew upon his head before he rose . And as the Dragon retains the ten horns after he is retired from the western Empire whose horns they are: so two horned Beast may keep both his horns & the woman both her wings & the two witnesses the number two after they are retired into one of the Empires.

But to remove all difficulty from these figures it is further {to} be considered that the two Empires were never so far parted but that they remained united in interest & {next} heirs to one another. When the western empire fell being invaded by barbarians, the Greek Emperor claimed a right to it & by vertue of that ri{g}ht recovered Afric from the Vandals & Italy from the Ostrogoths. And the western Emperors had the same right to the Eastern Empire. It is therefore to be conceived that even after the final division of the Empire the Dragon & Beast are {next} heirs to one anothers dominions & in token of that right partake of one anothers heads & horns And the same right which the Dragon & ten horned Beast have severally to be the whole Empire their Churches have under them ( the two horned Beast under the Dragon & the woman under the ten horned Beast ) to be the Church of the whole Empire & in token & claim of that right they partake of the binary division of the whole, the Woman by her two wings & the Beast by his two horns: for two wings & two horns have one & the same signification as is manifest by Daniels visions where the four wings of the Leopard & the four horns of the Goat signify one & the same division of the Greek Empire into four kingdoms. And much more is {it} to be conceived that the mystical body of Christ whether in the form of the son of man with two eyes & two leggs & seven stars in his hand, or in form of a Lamb with seven horns & seven eyes signifies the whole Church of Christ in all ages , & that after the division of the Empire, the septenary number is applied to the affairs of the Church in the Eastern Empire & the binary to her affairs in the western Empire without making a rent in Christs mystical body.

For the 144000 who were sealed our of all the twelve tribes of Israel stand on mount Sion with harps & sing a new song. They stand also on the sea of glass with harps and sing the song of Moses & the Lamb; & singing with musical instruments is prophesying <8r> ( 1 Sam. 10.5. 1 Chron. 25.1, 2, 3 ) & therefore they are Prophets, & Prophets are God's witnesses ( 2 Chron 24.19. ) From the time that the prophetic Book is opened all Gods people prophesy out of it. They are the mystical body of the mighty Angel who comes down from heaven cloathed with a cloud & the book open in his hand to prophesy out of it & sets his right foot upon the se{a}{æ} & his left foot upon the earth. A cloud is the symbol of a multitude. The Angel is cloathed with a cloud of witnesses or Prophets. The two horned Beast is called a fals Prophet & the great Whore is the woman Iezebel which calleth her self a Prophetess. In opposition to these two fals Prophets God has his two true Prophets in the earth & Sea represented by the two leggs of the mighty Angel: & these two true Prophets prophesy out of the sealed book from the time that the book is opened, & comprehend all Gods people in the whole Roman Empire, as well the 144000 as the two witnesses prophesy out of it against the Fals Prophet & against the fals Prophetess. The 144000 & the two witnesses are but two names of one & the same Church of God. And if after the final division of the Empire one of these names be used in describing the affairs of this Church within the eastern Empire & the other name be used in describing the affairs of this Church within the Western, yet for preserving the unity of Gods Church both these names are still to be taken for names derived from the whole & properly significant of the whole but applied to the several parts for the conveniencee of describing the affairs of those parts severally. Tis an usual figure to call a part by the name of the whole ( as when we call a dead body by the name of a man & every part of a river by the name of the river & the ten Tribes by the name of Israel ) & this figure is here made use of. As in the history of the Bible the ten tribes are distinguished from the two Tribes by the name of Israel & yet the two tribes are at the same time a part of Israel: so in the Apocalyps the people of God in the western empire may be distinguished from his people in the eastern by the names of the two witnesses & the 144000, & yet at the same time his people in the {e}astern empire may be a part of the two witnesses & his people in the western a part of the 144000. So far as the people of the eastern & western empires are distinguished from one another & considered in this prophesy distinctly, so far they are represented distinctly by the Dragon, Beast, Fals Prophet, Woman, 144000 & two witnesses: & so far as they are considered without distinction, so far each of those types extend to the people of the whole Empire without distinction. And therefore before the final division of the Empire, they extend each of them to the whole; after that division while they all continue together they are taken distinctly & particularly: but s{o} soon as any of them cease & thereby the distinction begins to be taken away the survivors so far as the distinction is taken away begin again to be taken generally & extend to the whole without distinction. During the reign of the Woman Babylon the great is her proper name written in her forehead, & She & the two horned Beast or fals Prophet are considered distinctly: but so soon as the Woman is burnt with fire, & this Babylon is fallen, the names of great Babylon & the fals Prophet extend to the whole without distinction. For after the woman is burnt its said that three unclean spirits like froggs came out of the mouth of the Dragon Beast & fals Prophet to gather the nations together to the battel of the great day of God Almighty, & the Beast & fals Prophet & kings of the earth ( or kings of the east whose way was prepared to this battel by the drying up of Euphrates ) were gathered together against the army of heaven, & the Beast & fals Prophet were taken & cast alive into the lake of fire & the remnant ( the kings of the earth or east ) were slain with the two edged sword. Here the woman is not named because she was burnt before, & the remnant of her seed who continue to worship the Beast & his Image are comprehended under the name of the fals Prophet he being next heir to the woman. It is also said that in this battel the great city was divided into three parts & great Babylon came in remembrance before God to give unto her the cup of the wine of the fiirceness of his wrath: & here great Babylon doth not signify the woman, for she was burnt before: but it <9r> comprehends all the three remaining parts of that great city the whole Roman Empire, two of which parts are cast alive into the lake of fire & the third is slain with the two edged sword. And in like manner, after the Beast is cas{t} alive into the lake of fire, the Dragon who is his next heir is no longer limited to the eastern Empire but extends into the whole. For when the Dragon that old serpent called the Devil & Satan is shut up in the bottomless pit that he should deceive the nations no more till a thousand years are expired, we are thereby to understand that the spirit of error ceases a thousand years in all the nations & kingdoms of this world which at the sounding of the seventh Trumpet become the kingdoms of our Lord & his Christ, that is, in all the kingdoms within the compass of the four monarchies. So also the two Witnesses which are opposed to the two winged Woman, upon the burning of that woman are no longer restrained to the western empire but ascending up to heaven in a cloud preach the everlasting gospel to every nation & kindred & tongue & people. And at the same time the 144000 grow into a great multitude which no man could number, of all nations & people & kindreds & tongues with palms in their hands, & thereby become one & the same Ch{u}rch catholic with the two witnesses. This is that Church called the Lamb's wife at whose marriage the kingdoms of the earth like sheep & oxen are mystically sacrificed by the victorious two edged sword & all the fowls of heaven invited to the marriage supper ( that is, the army on white horses ) are, not literally but mystically, filled with their flesh. For by this victory the Palm-bearing multitude come out of the great tribulation. So then the Woman & two Witnesses, & the two horned Beast & 144000 signify the Churches false & true of the whole Empire: but yet during the division of the Empire untill the fall of Babylon, for expressing that division & describing the actions of the churches in either part, the names of the two first are applied in a more especial manner to the Churches in the western empire & the names of the two first to the Churches in the eastern.

Now the two Witnesses prophesy in sackcloth 1260 days, & the Gentiles tread under foot the holy City 42 months & so long also the woman is fed in the Wilderness & the Beast acts as long. . The times are equal & by their equality are known to be coincident. When the Gentiles begin to tread under foot the holy city & the Beast begins to act & the Woman is arrived at her place of honour in the wilderness & begins to be fed there by the merchants of the earth, then the two Witnesses put on sackcloth. They prophesyed before, but now they begin to prophesy in mourning. But the period when they begin, is not yet known, because it involves the knowledge of the end of the 1260 days which are not yet expired. For the designe of this Prophesy is not to enable us to foreknow things, but by the fulfilling thereof to enable us to know certainly that it is a true prophesy & by consequence that there is a God above who foreknows all things & governs the world by his providence, & that Iesus Christ, the Lamb who was slain, rose again from the dead & received this prophesy from God & that he alone was worthy to receive it & became worthy by his death whereby he has also washed away the sins of his people in his blood & made them kings & Priests, having merited a kingdom for as many as he shall chuse to be his subjects when he comes to revive the dead & reward every man according to his works.

But tho the end of the 1260 days cannot yet be justly known, yet there is reason to believe that the days are not yet expiring. For the Gentiles which tread under foot the holy city, are called Gentiles with respect to their idolatry, & idolatry is not complete without the worshipping images. Invocation of dead men is a breach of the first commandment <10r> worshipping of Images is a breach of the second, & this is properly Idol-worship or idolatry. All the heathen Roman Empire w{e}re exceedingly addicted to the worship of images & this worship seems to be the principal character of heathenism {o}r gentilism, & therefore the Gentiles which tread under foot the Holy City might not begin to be Gentiles in the sense of the prophesy before the setting up of Idols in the Pantheon at Rome A.C. 610 , perhaps not before the setting up of Images in the Greek Empire { . } by the Empress Irene & her Council the second Council of Nice A.C. 787, perhaps not before the reign of the Empress Theodora & her son Michael & their setting up Images in the Greek Empire by the Council of Constantinople A.C. 842

The ten kingdoms into which the western Empire was divided were at first of different religions, & their kings were not all of them converted to the Roman catholic faith till about the beginning of the Woe-Trumpets & after that it was sometime before the generality of the people of some of those kingdoms were converted to that faith. And before such conversion, the tenn kings or kingdoms could not be said to have one mind & to agree & give their kingdom to the Beast, & before they did so the Beast might not be said to act in the sense of the Apocalyps nor to carry the Woman upon his back. & perhaps this agreement & acting might not begin before the erecting of the western Empire by Charles the great, & perhaps not before the breaking of this Empire into smaller parts at the death of his son A.C. 440, whereby the Pope might reign over them with more power than {o}ver the whole.

When the woman fled into the wilderness she did not presently arrive at her place or dignity where she is nourished 1260 days. In the Papacy of Gregory the great she was at the lowest ebb of fortune. Rome was then & or a long time after governed by a Duke & did not come into the hands of the Pope till the year 726. This Church did not obtein Peters patrimony & tiths for her nourishment till the reign of Pipin & Charles the great, nor arrived to its full dignity before the subjects of the Pope began to swear allegiance to him & he was declared above all humane judicature & took upon him to create Charles the great an Emperor A.C. 800 & even after that he grew still greater.

The little horn of Daniels fourth Beast had times & laws given into his hand for a time & times & half a time, which is the same interval of time with that of feeding the Woman in the wilderness. But before this horn began to act in such an absolute manner it is to be conceived that he established himself by rooting up three of the first horns which fell before him as he rose & this was not compassed before the fall of the kingdom{s} of the Lombards which was one of the three & fell A.C. 774.

And upon comparing all these things it seems to me that the witnesses did not p{u}t on sackcloth before the beginning of the Woe-trumpets & most probably not till some time after.
